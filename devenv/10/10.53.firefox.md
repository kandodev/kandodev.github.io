## Vimium shortcuts
* H go backwards
* L go forwards
* X restore closed tab
* o (O in new tab) open url, bookmark, is history entry
* T search through open tabs
* / to search. v to enter visual mode -> can use y to yank to clipboard
* yy to copy current url to clipboard
